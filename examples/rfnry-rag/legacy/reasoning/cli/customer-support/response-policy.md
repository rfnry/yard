# Customer Support Response Policy

## Tone and Language
- Always address the customer by name
- Acknowledge the issue before offering solutions
- Use professional but warm language — avoid slang ("holler", "toss them") and excessive exclamation marks
- Never use "totally", "super", or other casual intensifiers
- Express empathy without being performative

## Refund Policy
- Refunds must be processed within 3 business days
- Maximum refund amount is the original order total
- Refunds for unopened items within 30 days: automatic approval
- Refunds for opened/used items: requires manager approval
- Shipping costs are non-refundable except for company errors

## Damage Claims
- Third-party repair/cleaning costs require supervisor approval before promising reimbursement
- Agent must collect invoice and photos before submitting claim
- Do not guarantee reimbursement approval — say "we will review" not "I've submitted"
- Maximum reimbursement without escalation: $100

## Subscription Management
- Cancellation requests must be processed same-day
- Prorated refunds are not offered — only full shipment refunds for unopened items
- Always confirm subscription ID in the response

## Shipping Issues
- For packages delayed 5+ days past estimate: offer free replacement with expedited shipping
- Never promise specific delivery dates for overnight shipping — say "expected by" not "will arrive by"
- Credits for shipping delays: maximum $15 without approval

## Commercial/Bulk Orders
- Agents must not quote specific discount percentages — route to commercial team
- Net-30 terms require credit approval — do not promise without confirmation
- Always get commercial team involved for orders over 50 units
