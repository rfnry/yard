# Green Thai Curry with Chicken

Bangkok-style green curry. Thinner than the Western restaurant version, more aromatic.

## INGREDIENTS

- 500 g chicken thigh
- 60 g green curry paste
- 400 ml coconut milk
- 200 ml chicken stock
- 2 kaffir lime leaves
- 30 g palm sugar
- 30 ml fish sauce
- 100 g Thai eggplants
- 50 g bamboo shoots
- 20 g Thai basil
- 1 red chili
- 15 ml vegetable oil

## STEPS

1. Slice chicken into 2-cm pieces. Quarter the eggplants.
2. Heat oil in a wok. Fry the curry paste for 90 seconds until fragrant.
3. Add half the coconut milk; reduce until it splits and the oil rises.
4. Add chicken; coat in the paste. Pour in stock and remaining coconut milk.
5. Add lime leaves, palm sugar, and fish sauce. Simmer 8 minutes.
6. Add eggplants and bamboo shoots; simmer 5 more minutes.
7. Off heat, stir in Thai basil and sliced chili. Serve over jasmine rice.

## TIPS

- Splitting the coconut milk is the technique. It's how you know the paste has bloomed.
- Palm sugar > brown sugar. The flavor is round in a way refined sugar cannot match.
- Vegetarian: replace chicken with firm tofu and fish sauce with light soy + a pinch of salt.
