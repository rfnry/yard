# Chocolate Eclairs

Classic French chocolate eclairs with vanilla pastry cream.

## INGREDIENTS

- 250 ml water
- 100 g unsalted butter
- 150 g all-purpose flour
- 4 large eggs
- 5 g salt
- 10 g sugar
- 500 ml whole milk
- 100 g sugar
- 4 large egg yolks
- 40 g cornstarch
- 1 vanilla bean
- 200 g dark chocolate
- 200 ml heavy cream

## STEPS

1. Bring water, butter, salt, and sugar to a boil. Add flour all at once and stir vigorously until the dough pulls from the pan.
2. Off heat, beat in eggs one at a time until the dough is smooth and glossy.
3. Pipe 12-cm logs onto a parchment-lined sheet. Bake at 200 C for 20 minutes, then 180 C for 15 minutes.
4. For the cream, scald milk with the split vanilla bean. Whisk yolks, sugar, and cornstarch. Temper, return to heat, and cook until thick.
5. Cool the cream covered in plastic touching the surface. Pipe into eclairs through three small holes in the bottom.
6. Melt chocolate with cream over a bain-marie. Dip the tops of each eclair and let set.

## TIPS

- Eclairs need a hot start to puff. Don't open the oven before the 20-minute mark.
- Cream is too soft? Cook one minute longer next time.
- For mocha eclairs, add 8 g of instant espresso to the cream while scalding the milk.
