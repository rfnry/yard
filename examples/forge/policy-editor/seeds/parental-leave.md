# Parental Leave Policy

## Eligibility

Employees with at least 6 months of continuous service are eligible for parental leave following the birth, adoption, or fostering of a child.

## Duration

Eligible employees may take up to 12 weeks of paid parental leave. Leave must be taken within the 12 months following the qualifying event.

## Pay

Parental leave is paid at 100% of base salary. Variable compensation (bonus, commission) is excluded.

## Coordination with Other Leave

Parental leave runs concurrently with statutory entitlements where applicable. It does not stack with short-term disability for the same period.

## Approval

Notify your manager and HR at least 30 days in advance when foreseeable. The HR partner confirms eligibility and pay treatment in writing.
