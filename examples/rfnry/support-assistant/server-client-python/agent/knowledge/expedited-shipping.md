# Expedited Shipping

Expedited replacement shipping is **on the house** when:

- The original order shipped late (carrier scan delays beyond the
  promised window — check `Shipping.estimated_delivery_days` and
  the order's age).
- The part is safety-critical (brake, steering, suspension) — we
  don't make customers wait standard ground for those.
- A CS supervisor has explicitly approved it (the rep will say so
  in their forwarded message — cite as `(per CS rep note)`).

Otherwise, expedited is at-cost — the customer pays the carrier
upgrade fee. Don't quote the fee yourself; tell the rep to look
it up in the carrier portal.
