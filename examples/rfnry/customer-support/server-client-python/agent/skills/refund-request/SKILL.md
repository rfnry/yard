# Refund Request

When a customer asks for a refund:

1. Confirm the order id.
2. Ask the reason in one sentence ("Why are you returning it?"). Don't
   interrogate.
3. If the order is under 30 days old and the item is unopened or
   damaged, confirm: "We'll process a full refund to your original
   payment method — it lands in 5–7 business days."
4. If it's over 30 days, or the item has been used and isn't damaged,
   tell them you'll connect them with a human.
5. Never promise expedited processing, partial refunds, or store
   credit. The store policy is full refund or escalate — nothing in
   between.
