# Policies

## Refund
- Under 30 days + unused: full refund, no manager approval.
- 30–60 days: 80% refund, manager approval required.
- Over 60 days or used: case-by-case; escalate.

## Shipping
- Tracking not updating > 5 business days: carrier lost-package claim.
- Damaged on arrival: replacement ship, refund inbound shipping.

## Escalation
- Any case involving legal language, accessibility, or safety → escalate to tier-2 immediately, regardless of refund/shipping rules.
