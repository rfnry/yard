# Refund Policy

Full refund is offered when:

- The customer asks for one explicitly **and** the order is within
  30 days of delivery **and** the part is unused or damaged.
- A replacement isn't possible (e.g. discontinued part).

Refunds always go to the **original payment method** (`Payments.method`).
Processing window:

- Card payments — 5–7 business days to land.
- ACH — 7–10 business days.
- Net-30 / wholesale — credit memo applied to the next invoice; no
  cash refund.

Partial refunds are **not** an option. Either we refund the full
order line or we don't. If a customer asks for a partial, the rep
should kick it to a CS supervisor — flag it in **Open questions**.
