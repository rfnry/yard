# Admissibility — Common Challenges

The records pulled here are admissible if authenticated, but
opposing counsel will commonly challenge:

- **Hearsay (FRE 802):** public-records hearsay exception (FRE
  803(8)) covers most of these, but the record must be from a
  public office and within the office's duty to record.
- **Authentication (FRE 901):** a printout from a vendor isn't
  self-authenticating. A certified copy from the source system is
  (FRE 902(4)).
- **Best evidence (FRE 1002):** when content of a document is at
  issue, opposing counsel can demand the original. The vendor copy
  is a derivative.

For investigative work this is fine. Before any filing, swap the
vendor printouts for certified copies — that switch is what the
`Followups` line is reminding the investigator to schedule.
