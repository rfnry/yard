# Remote Work Policy

## Scope

Applies to all full-time employees in approved remote-eligible roles.

## Eligibility

Roles are designated remote-eligible by the people-team in coordination with the role owner. Eligibility is recorded on the role description, not the individual.

## Equipment

The company provides a laptop, headset, and one external monitor. Additional peripherals are reimbursed up to USD 250 per year.

## Hours

Core hours are 10:00–16:00 in the employee's declared time zone. Outside core hours, employees set their own schedule.

## Security

Use only company-provided devices for company data. Connect through the corporate VPN when accessing internal services.
